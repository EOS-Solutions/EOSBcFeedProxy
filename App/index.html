EOS Solutions NuGet Feeds for Business Central

The URLs for the NuGet feeds are built in the following format:

/{BC version}/{Type}

Where {BC version} is the version of Business Central, and {Type} can be one of the following:

  • Release: No authentication required
  • Prerelease: Requires MSAL authentication
  • Test: Requires MSAL authentication

For example, to access the NuGet feed for Business Central 25 Release, you would use the following URL:

myawesomeeosapps.com/25/Release/index.json
